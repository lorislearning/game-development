You already know how to change the size of your world and window together, but what if you want to keep your window small but make your world much larger?

You can change the size of your world in your createFunction() with:

game.world.setBounds(0, 0, 400, 1920);

The setBounds() method takes in four arguments:

  1. The x-value of the top-left corner of the world
  2. The y-value of the top-left corner of the world
  3. The width of the world in pixels
  4. The height of the world in pixels

You also might want to make the camera follow your player as it moves around in the world. You can do this in your createFunction() with:

game.camera.follow(player);

The follow() method only takes in one argument - the sprite that the camera is going to follow.
